Still got questions?

There may be bad sledges, but no bad questions, hopefully these answer all yours.

Where are the games hosted?

+

All games will be hosted at Playpoint Blacktown!

How many games per week?

+

Every week, you get to play two games which equate to an hours worth of play.

What grade suits me?

+

First grade is for those who are looking for the highest level of competition ICL has to offer. Second Grade is for those returning to the sport and want a mix of competition and fun. Third Grade is for purely for fun and encourages team collaboration and player bonding.

Can I still play if I’m an individual?

+

Absolutely! Get into contact with our team using any of the socials below and we will happily find you a team!

Where do I find my match stats?

+

All stats will be updated on Cricheroes for Juniors, and PlayHQ for Seniors.
